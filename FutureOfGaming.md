## The future of online gaming and NFTs

Firstly, my views as an **indie dev**.

Did you know that in order to publish your game on the steam platform, you must pay 100 dollars as an "admin fee" AND steam takes 30% of your sales along with exclusivity to steam.
This might sound horrible, but steam kind of invented the indie dev space with their steam platform. With the use of wishlists and your games store page,
indie devs now could market their games incredibly easy. Steam was released in \*1 **2003** (twenty years ago) But with time, the internet changed and the game development process changed with it. Now you have Unity and Unreal engine.

> **Note:** This should not be concidered financial advice. Always do your own research.
